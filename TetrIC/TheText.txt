                                         The TetrIC project was made by Fredrik Andersson, Torbjörn Aronsson, Mats Christianson, Mathias Johansson, Jan-Peter Nilsson and Mattias Severson. The ASIC was done at the Lund Institute of Technology at the department of Electroscience with lots of help from Anders Berkeman and Martin Nilsson. The ASIC project took more or less 4000 man hours to complete and is fitted on 25 square millimeters of silicon. TetrIC the game was programmed by Fredrik Andersson.
